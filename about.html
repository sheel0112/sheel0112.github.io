About

I got into product to make complex systems feel simple and dependable. Working in sports trading exposed me to high-stakes risk decisions, where speed and safety have to coexist. That mindset now guides how I build fintech and banking products: clear controls, fast feedback loops, and user journeys that hold up under pressure.

I'm excited by fintech because it's where trust, regulation, and user experience collide. The opportunity to build safer, smarter tools for customers and internal teams — across markets and time zones — is what motivates me to keep learning and leading.

  • Values: collaborative, calm under pressure, and relentlessly curious.
  • Mindset: global perspective from work across the UK, EU, and North America.
  • Goal: guide teams toward risk-aware, user-first delivery in fintech and banking.
