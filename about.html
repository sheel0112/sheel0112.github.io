About

I’m a Product Owner working at the intersection of risk, data, and engineering. I focus on building tools that make complex financial systems feel simple, intuitive, and reliable for the people who use them day to day.

Most of my recent work has been in live trading environments, where pricing, risk, and operational workflows need to be both fast and safe. I enjoy translating technical depth into clear user journeys, joining up modelling, data engineering, backend services, and frontend platforms into products that actually help people make better decisions.
