About me

Why I got into product, and why I'm leaning hard into fintech.

Why product?

I moved into product to bridge the space between engineering detail and the real-world pressure of trading desks. I enjoy making complicated systems usable, reliable, and fast for the people who depend on them.

Sports trading risk taught me to deliver under time pressure, measure exposure in real time, and communicate decisions with clarity. Those habits transfer directly to regulated fintech environments.

Fintech and banking excite me because the stakes are higher: more users, tighter controls, and opportunities to build trust through great tooling. I'm looking for teams that care about safety and velocity in equal measure.

How I work

  • Collaborative: open comms with engineering, data, design, and ops.
  • Calm under pressure: comfortable shipping when risk is high and time is short.
  • Curious: always pairing with engineers and analysts to understand the edge cases.
  • Global outlook: experienced across UK, EU, and North America with a bias for inclusive teams.
